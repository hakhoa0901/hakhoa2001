NOUNS
A noun represents a person,place,animal,thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows:

Proper :It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea of thing.

Commom :It is the name possessed by things in general.

Gollective :It is the name representing a group of things .

Abstract:It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions of actions.

PRONOUNS
A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronouns are classified as follows :

Personal pronouns :It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.

Interrogative pronouns:It stands as the answer to the question abouy a person, place of a thing.

Indefinite pronouns:It is used to refer to a person, thing of a place that are unknown.

Relative pronouns:It relates to a noun, pronoun, word of expression preceding it is the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.

Bemonstrative pronouns:A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it

Reflexive pronouns:It usually follows a noun of a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
